Getting Started

Press to display the mode-selection screen.

Select a mode with , then press to begin.
COMPETITION
Competition is a one-player story mode where you compete against several computer-controlled opponents. You win when your opponent's third column (from the left) reaches the top of the screen. Don't let this happen to you!

Competition has three modes: EASY (three practice stages are available, and hints are on), NORMAL (all stages are available, but hints are turned off), and HARD (same as NORMAL, but you start on Stage 4).

1P VS. 2P
Play against a friend in this competitive versus mode. Game play is the same as in Competition.

PRACTICE
In this mode, you have no opponent. Play continues until the third column from the left reaches the top of the screen. Try for a high score!

OPTIONS
EXIT Leave the Options screen.
VS. COM LEVEL Choose the speed and CPU difficulty for 1-player games. There are four speeds to choose from: EASY to HARDEST.
1P VS. 2P MODE Choose the number of matches for each game in 2-player mode.
SAMPLING This will turn off the voices during game play.
FACE In 2-player mode, you can choose to display the image of your opponent on their screen in the front, in the back, or not at all.
BUTTON ASSIGNMENT Assign commands to the buttons of your choice.
INPUT TEST Verify that your controller is working properly.
CUSTOM Listen to the game audio tracks (SOUND, MUSIC, VOICE), change the audio options (SOUND MODE), move your opponent's picture on his screen (FACE POSITION), or make him sweat (SWEAT)!
