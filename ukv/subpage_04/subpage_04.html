Getting Started

Press on the title screen to proceed to the file-selection screen. Use to scroll through the options, then press to confirm your selection and move to the game-mode-selection screen.

Choose either ONE Player (to play by yourself), TWO Player Team (to play with another player cooperatively), or TWO Player Contest (to take turns with another player and compete to see who can finish the most levels in the shortest time). Choose Copy Game to copy data and Erase Game to erase game data. Data that has been erased cannot be restored, so please be careful.

During multiplayer games, each player will need a controller compatible with the Virtual Console. For more information, check the Wii Operations Manual.
