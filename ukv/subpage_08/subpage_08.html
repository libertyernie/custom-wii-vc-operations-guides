Course Elements
Some courses in Special Tee Shot include certain features also found in Kirby's Dream Course™. Consult the Kirby's Dream Course™ Operations Guide for more information.
Features
Button
 Magnetizes the hole, making it easier to hit the ball into, and toggles the lights on and off. When the lights are off, extra power-ups might be revealed (see "Power-Ups" below).
Switch
 Adds or removes certain elements of the course, such as water or bridge areas.
Launcher
 Sends the ball in a straight line across the course until it hits an obstacle or goes into the hole.
Hole
 The target at the end of the course. The goal of each course is to hit the ball into the hole.
Obstacles
Potted Plant
 A simple obstacle to avoid by hitting the ball over or around.
Blob
 Captures the ball when hit; equivalent to a shot out of bounds.
Bumper
 Causes the ball to ricochet at high speeds.
Power-Ups
Power Shot
 Turns the next fly shot into a Power Shot, allowing you to hit the ball up to twice as far. Can be combined with the Special Shot.
Special Shot
 Turns the next fly shot into a Special Shot. Once a Special Shot hits the ground, it will be sent in a straight line across the course until it hits an obstacle or goes into the hole. Can be combined with the Power Shot.
